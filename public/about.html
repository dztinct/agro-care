About The App!

This app is developed to support agriculture in rural areas in a digital way,
to bring enlightenment to rural farmers by notifying them digitally about
diseases both mild and deadly in plants and animals so as to aid great
farm yield produce from the rural agricultural sector and also to curb loss,
mismanagement and mishandling of farm produce.
